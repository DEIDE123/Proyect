About Us

EIDE is a non-profit project promoted by students of the Nazaret Educational Centre with the aim of organising different events about computer science and different outings. Our events are aimed at all kinds of people, whether they are young or old. We want people to be aware that there are many types of jobs related to computer science and for this reason we believe that this type of talks, meetings .... are essential. Thus, from our project we want to make known in a clear and direct way how we can tackle this challenge within an educational environment. EIDE arises from the idea of making our work and our future known and promoting different professional opportunities.

At EIDE we try to make our events as fun as possible. We have a small variety of facilities in which to hold our events. The Nazareth Educational Centre and the Kursaal Elkargunea, give us the opportunity to expand and reach more people. Our team is made up of four students from the Nazareth Educational Centre.
